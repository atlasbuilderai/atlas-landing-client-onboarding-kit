The problem

For many freelancers and small agencies, onboarding still happens across random docs, invoice links, forgotten checklist items, and back-and-forth emails. That slows down delivery and makes the business feel less polished than it should.

Scattered admin

Client details, files, contracts, invoices, kickoff notes, and next steps live in different places.

Repeated setup work

You recreate the same onboarding flow every time instead of reusing one clean process.

Missed steps

Small but important tasks slip through: asset requests, kickoff prep, reminders, approvals, and handoff notes.

Client confusion

The client isn’t always sure what happens next, which creates extra friction and unnecessary questions.
